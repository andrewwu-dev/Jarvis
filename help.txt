Here are some things Jarvis can do:

Play Music:
    - "play 'song_name' "
    - "Hey Jarvis, play 'song_name' "

Pause Music:
    - "pause music"
    - "Jarvis, pause the music"

Stop Music:
    - "stop music"
    - "Jarvis stop the music"

Resume Music:
    - "resume music"
    - "Jarvis, resume the music"

Get Current Weather Condition:
    - "weather"
    - "get me today's weather"
    - "Hey Jarvis, what's the weather like?"

Get Forecasted Weather Condition(1-10 days from today):
    - "weather 1 day from now"
    - "Jarvis, weather 5 days from now"
